Wysa

Approach effectiveness

Wysa has a record of clinical evidence with peer-reviewed trials and efficacy studies conducted by prominent institutions.6 It was found to be more effective than in-person psychological counseling. As a result, it was awarded the FDA Breakthrough Device Designation for its diagnosis of chronic pain brought about by depression, and anxiety. Unlike most mental health applications which apply techniques from cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), Wysa borrows techniques from other scientifically-proven therapies such as dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), and mediation. DBT, in particular, is targeted towards individuals who have difficulty regulating their emotions.7 Through a series of group sessions, the patient learns to embrace their whole self, while realizing that there is always room for improvement. Every individual, including the therapist, is given equal treatment to maximize the potential for personal development through discussion, empathetic listening, and inner reflection. Although Wysa cannot fully replace the face-to-face experience, it tries its very best to connect patients to mental health coaches to guide sessions.

We give Wysa a functionality effectiveness rating of 8 out of 10.

Functionality

Although the AI coach is marketed as using proprietary artificial intelligence and natural language processing algorithms. In actuality, the agent is a decision-tree.8 The chatbot is preprogrammed with rules and responses. Since the chatbot is not a generative or adaptive model, its functionality is very limited as it cannot be continually trained. As such, we see very little opportunities for improvement and potential for widespread use. 

We give Wysa a functionality rating of 1 out of 10.

Security

Wysa empowers users to have full control over the data they provide.8 After completing a session, users have the ability to delete their data. Otherwise, the data is retained for 24 hours, but Wysa does not claim ownership of the data. Wysa does not require user registration, providing a nickname is sufficient for personalizing the user experience. Furthermore, the AI algorithms redact any information revealing a person’s identity, which anonymizes the data. On top of that, no human has access to the stored data, so it cannot be abused. Because the AI coach is a pre programmed decision tree, user data is not stored for training purposes.

We give Wysa a security rating of 10 out of 10.

Affordability

Wysa has two pricing options:

  • premium (includes tools): $99.99 per year
  • premium plus (includes coaching & tools): $99.99 per month

We give it an affordability ranking of 7 out of 10.
