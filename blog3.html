7 Cups

7 Cups is an on-demand emotional support service. It has been featured in the Wall Street Journal, the New Yorker, TechCrunch, and USA Today. Peer-reviewed publications have concluded that 7 Cups is an effective platform for developing coping strategies and reducing stress by immersing individuals in an environment filled with kinship.

  • What is active listening? A set of communication skills that demonstrate empathy, compassion, and respect. It goes beyond simply listening to what someone has to say. It requires effort, and utmost concentration & understanding. The listener is constantly asking questions and seeking clarification, as well as acknowledging the speaker’s feelings. Research has shown that active listening, causes the speaker and listener’s brain to synchronize, leading to better understanding and comprehension.

Like most mental health applications, 7 Cups provides a chatbot and a 24/7 anonymous, emotional support helpline. While not much information has been provided, it purportedly uses cutting-edge technology including machine learning to provide a limited chat capabilities. The feature that distinguishes this application from its contenders is that it hosts a forum. This form connects users to hundreds of listeners who come from all walks of life and have diverse experiences. All listeners must complete 7 Cup’s Active Listening program, which is an online course teaching compassionate communication. They must receive a perfect score on assessment. According to the privacy policy, 7 Cups stores a variety of personal information. This information, along with first and third-party cookies are collected to operate, and continuously improve the site and its services. Under no circumstances is data misused. Personal information may be shared, for instance, with health professionals out of concern for the user's wellbeing or data compliant service providers so that they can provide essential services for the platform. At any point in time, users are able to request, view, and delete their collected data. Otherwise, data is retained until the account is deactivated or been inactive for longer than a year, at which point, the data is immediately purged from the system. Not to mention, the user's interactions with listeners and community members are kept strictly confidential. Measures have been placed on each form of communication to ensure the user's identity and privacy are respected. Listeners are only aware of information that has been disclosed to them directly by the user. Messages are automatically deleted within 30 days, and posts on community forums are maintained by administrators.

As such, we rate 7 Cup's privacy 9 out of 10.
