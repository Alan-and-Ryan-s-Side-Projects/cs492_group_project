Sanity & Self

S&S is an app that allows users to have focus on themselves, and offers 1 on 1 coaching sessions with professions of various fields such as comedy, sex, wellness, meditation and mental health. 

Effectiveness

Many users have expressed that they’ve been positively impacted by S&S, be it the decrease in stress while using the app, finding opportunities for personal growth, or something as simple as working out. Some users have expressed discontent with how S&S targets their users. S&S would use terms such as “girlboss” to refer to the user, and thus alienate most men and many older women who do not identify with such pleasantries. With a 4.8 star rating on the app store, we see that the overwhelming sentiment favors the app. However, there are no formal studies or research conducted with S&S which suggest that this app will be more effective than others at mental health care.

We give S&S a 4/10 for effectiveness

Functionality

S&S has a ton of content, there are specialized packages for everything self related. Programs include ‘setting boundaries’, ‘prioritize yourself’ and ‘know yourself’.16 Then there are tools such as Mood Tracker, Guided Journal, Relaxation, and Reminder. There seems to be no end to what you can do on this app. Each feature ties the themes around this app very well, to hold yourself accountable, regulate your mood and have some sort of immediate feedback even if there’s no one around to validate your feelings. However the centerpiece of this app is its audio programs. These are short sessions no longer than 20 minutes each, on the topics of empowerment, self-discovery, healing, mindfulness, and stress-release; they can be guided workout periods that focus on strength, stretching and cross training. There is also music for sleep, focus and stress. Of course we must also talk about 1 on 1 coaching for various topics. You thought that is all? Nope, you can also choose to connect with others in this cohesive S&S community, sharing your woes and other personal information. As great as it is to have so many things to do on the app, some users also feel that they have been distracted by the options. Nonetheless, the vast majority of users will find something to enjoy on the app, be it just music, self improvement or community involvement. 

We give S&S a 8/10 score on functionality

Security

The privacy policy for S&S is very vague about the kind of information that is collected. It then didn’t come at too much of a surprise that personal information is sold to Facebook according to the consumer report.17 

We give S&S a 2/10 score on security

Affordability

$59.99 Annually to access audio library

Text chat costs $39.99/week to $49.99/week. Video appointments cost between $60-$200 for 30 minute sessions depending on the expert.

Although the price is not too out of the ordinary for audio subscription and it being entirely optional, those who wish to find 1 on 1 appointments and texting would find themselves requiring much more capital expenditure. 

We give S&S 3/10 on affordability
